Stained glass has always been a passion - It's often called 
'painting with light'



Greenart is the website for Scottish artist Richard Green. Richard is a 
painter but now works with stained glass creating free standing stained glass 
panels from his studio in Blackness, West Lothian. He also produces small pieces of glass jewellery.
The <a href="http://www.green-art.co.uk/galleries.php?gallery=1">stained 
glass galleries</a> on this website show Richard's latest work and there is also information on <a 
href="http://www.green-art.co.uk/commissions.html">commissioning an artwork</a> from the artist. You can also <a 
href="http://www.green-art.co.uk/richard-green.html">read more about Richard</a> 
or how he <a href="http://www.green-art.co.uk/work.html">designs his stained 
glass artwork</a> .
